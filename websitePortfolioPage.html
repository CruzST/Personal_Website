PIG
  • Pig is a dice game where the object of the game is to rack up points. A player begins their turn by rolling dice. Any number other than a 1 is added to their "current score". If the player chooses to "hold", their current score is added to their total. The next player then goes and repeats. If a player rolls a 1 on their turn, then any points they accumulated during their turn are lost and the next player goes.
