About Me

I'm an Engineer

Prior towards my path of Computer Science, I was a road designer for a Civil Engineering Firm. I studied hard for the Fundemental Engineering Exam and earned my Engineer in Training License as I quickly rose the ranks and became the lead designer for Municipal Roadways within the office. I have always held an interest in technology however my interest in construction and design was greater at the time. When designing roads I worked intensively with autoCAD and Civil3D, during that time I had the chance to use programing fundamentals by creating and using autoLisps to perform repetitive tasks. As my interest in computers and programming reignited and plenty of online resources I decided to make the switch to Computer Science. So far, I like it!

I (will be) three degree student

I previously attended Drexel University in Philadelphia and obtained my Bachelor's in Civil and Architectural Engineering. Currently a student enrolled at Oregon State University I am a part of their Online Program for my third and final Bachelors. A third into the cirriculum OSU has provided me with plenty of knowledge and more to come. I look forward to the rest of the program and starting my career in Computer Science. It is hard to say as of now what I want to do as a career but I think I'll find my way as I take more courses.
